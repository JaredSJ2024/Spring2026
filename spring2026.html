Winter 2026 Recap
Thank you to all the fencers who competed at the Midwestern Fencing Championships this past weekend! It was a big success and the Men's Foil Team was able to secure the Gold with the Women's Foil Team securing Silver! In addition, shout out our Co-President Oscar Chen for reaching 3rd in Individual Senior Men's Sabre, Ugonna Egbychulan for winning Gold in Individual Senior Women's Epée, Adam Katwan for winning Gold in Individual's Men's Foil, John Chen for Seventh Place in Individual Senior Men's Foil, Foil Captain Charlotte Landman for securing Gold in Indivdiual Senior Women's Foil, and Co-Vice Presidents Izzy Lee and Leslie Xiao for winning 3rd and 5th in Individual Senior Women's Foil, respectively.
